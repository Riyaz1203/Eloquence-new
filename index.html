Embrace the world of coding and debugging with open arms. Code is your canvas, bugs are your puzzles, and solutions are your creations. Welcome to the journey of digital problem-solving and innovation!!!

➢Total of 5 problem statements will be provided.
➢The problem statement will be from domains: [Sorting,Array manipulation,Searching,Pattern,String].
➢The internet access will be denied for this round.
